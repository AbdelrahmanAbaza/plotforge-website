Overview

What is PlotForge?

PlotForge is a dedicated data analysis application for engineering, quality, and analytics teams who work daily with CSV and Excel files. It is designed to help organisations standardise their analysis workflows, shorten investigation time, and improve traceability across projects and programs.

By combining an intuitive interface with powerful plotting and region analysis tools, PlotForge enables teams to move beyond ad-hoc spreadsheets and establish a repeatable, professional approach to data review.

  • Rapid import and handling of large CSV & Excel datasets
  • Plot multiple signals on a common X-axis for direct comparison
  • Interactive selection zones with automatic Min / Max / Œî / Avg calculations
  • One-click export of highlighted regions and plots to CSV or image for reporting
  • Reusable templates and session saving to support consistent, repeatable analysis across teams

Story

Why I built PlotForge

PlotForge was created by an engineer who spent years analysing test and measurement data in spreadsheets and heavy analysis tools. The same patterns appeared again and again:

  • Time lost manually building plots from CSV or Excel files.
  • Inconsistent analysis between projects and team members.
  • Complex tools being overkill for day-to-day investigations.

PlotForge is an attempt to fix that: a focused, engineer-friendly tool that keeps the workflows you already know (CSV, Excel, regions, plots) but makes them faster, more repeatable, and easier to share with your team.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is PlotForge for?

PlotForge is aimed at engineers and analysts who regularly work with measurement, test, or log data stored in CSV or Excel files. Typical users include test & validation engineers, quality engineers, and analytics teams.

What can I do during the 14-day trial?

The trial is fully featured. You can import your own data, explore all plotting and analysis tools, create regions, export reports, and save/load sessions to see how PlotForge fits into your workflow.

Does PlotForge require scripting or coding?

No. PlotForge is built to be used directly by engineers without writing code. Most operations are performed via menus, dialogs, and interactive tools such as cursors, regions, and selection zones.
