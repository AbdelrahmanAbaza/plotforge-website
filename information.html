Purpose

PlotForge was created with the goal of providing engineers and analysts with a simple, intuitive tool to process and visualize their data. We aim to reduce the time spent managing and interpreting data, while increasing accuracy and providing valuable insights.

Objectives

  • Make data analysis simpler and faster for professionals.
  • Provide a user-friendly, intuitive interface for engineers and analysts.
  • Offer powerful features to handle CSV and Excel data seamlessly.
  • Enable quick plotting and analysis without the need for extensive technical skills.
